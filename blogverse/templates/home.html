Frequently Asked Questions

What is this blog platform about? ➤
This platform lets users publish their blogs, read others' content, and engage through comments and likes.
How can I post a new blog? ➤
Once registered and logged in, you'll find a "Create Blog" button in your dashboard where you can write and publish.
Can I edit or delete my blog after posting? ➤
Yes! Navigate to "My Blogs" section in your dashboard to edit or delete your posts anytime.
Is there a comment feature for readers? ➤
Absolutely. Readers can leave comments under any blog post, and authors can moderate them.
How can I report inappropriate content? ➤
Every post has a "Report" button to flag it. Our moderators review reports to maintain community guidelines.
